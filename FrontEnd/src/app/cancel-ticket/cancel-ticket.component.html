About Us

Millions of people travel on buses daily in India. Buses are the most important means of transportation for the common man as these provide the close-to-home connectivity. If you want to know about the buses plying on different routes across India, you can opt for BuzzWings.

BuzzWings is a platform made by young entrepreneurs.It is basically an online bus ticketing platform that will transform bus travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using buses.
