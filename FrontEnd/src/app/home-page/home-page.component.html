Marina Beach

Chennai, TamilNadu

Established:- 2017

The Adiyogi statue is a 34-metre-tall, 45-metre-long and 25-metre-wide steel statue of the Hindu deity Shiva with Thirunamam at Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. It is recognized by the Guinness World Records as the "Largest Bust Sculpture” in the world.
